About TigerCrush

TigerCrush is a platform that enables Princeton undergraduates to see which of their fellow students that they have a crush on are crushing back on them. If TigerCrush identifies such a match, that is, a student you're crushing on is crushing back on you, both of you will be notified via email of the match, and it's up to you to take things from there!

TigerCrush was developed by Alan Ding '22, Oleg Golev '22, and Gerald Huang '22. The development team would like to acknowledge that this application was inspired by GoodCrush, a now-defunct platform created by Princeton alum Josh Weinstein '09 that once served a similar purpose. We would also like to acknowledge the original poster of the Tiger Confession (#15437) that dug up this decade-old relic from the depths of the Internet.
